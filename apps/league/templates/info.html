What is Codeleague?


Codeleague is a platform created from the idea that everyone could be able to participate in competitions in the field of computer science.

But that's not all because everyone will also be able to create their own competition!


Can I participate in competitions?


Everyone can take part in these events. You can participate alone or with a team of maximum 4 members.

If you want to meet new people you have the possibility to create a free group, which means that
when you create your group you able that other people with no group could join you.

How can I create a competition?


Everyone is able to create their own competition. It's very easy! As we want that everyone could take part in this platform, everyone can create a competition. The only thing you have to do is is click on "Create a competition" on the home page. Afterwards, you will only have to fill out a very short form, and that's all!
