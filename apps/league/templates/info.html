What is Codeleague?


Codeleague is a platform created from the idea that everyone could be able to participate in competitions in the field of computer science.

But that's not all because everyone will also be able to create their own competition!


Can I participate in competitions?


Everyone can take part in these events. You can participate alone or with a team of maximum 4 members.

If you want to meet new people you have the possibility to create a free group, which means that when you
create your group you able that other people with no group could join you.


How can I create a competition?


As we want that everyone could take part in this platform, everyone is able to create their own competition.
It's very easy!

The only thing you have to do is click on "Create a competition" on the home page. Afterwards, you will
only have to fill out a very short form, and that's all!


How the winners will be announced?


Even every competition will have different challenges. Despite this, the way to evaluate every group and
proclaim a winner will be the same.

In all competitions, every challenge will have a score. When you deliver any challenge, depending on how
you have solved it, your group will add more or less points to the scoreboard.

During the competition a ranking will be continuously updated, and when the competition finishes, the
ranking will be updated with the final scores.

We will contact the winners personally to make sure they receive their prize!
